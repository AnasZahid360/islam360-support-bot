## Complete Feature List

### THE HOLY QURAN

**Reading Modes:**
- Read by Surah (114 chapters)
- Read by Parah/Juz (30 sections)
- 15-Line Quran — traditional multi-verse page layout
- 16-Line PDF Quran — high-quality PDF with annotation capability (added iOS v5.1.0)
- Word-by-Word zoom option (added v5.0.1)
- Color-Coded Tajweed Quran — Tajweed rules highlighted visually, certified by the Research & Registration Department of Auqaf after verification and proofreading

**Translations (25+):**
- Languages: English, Urdu, Hindi, Roman Urdu, Punjabi (Gurmukhi — added v5.0.1), Kashmiri, Seraiki, Potohari, Gojri, Persian, and more (30+ languages total)
- Named translators: Muhammad Juna Garhi, Noor ul Amin, Mufti Taqi Usmani, Tahir-ul-Qadri, Amin Ahsan Islahi, Allama Hasan Rizvi, Nighat Hashmi, Abul Alaa Moududi, Kanzul Iman
- Audio translations: Kashmiri, Seraiki, Potohari, Gojri, Persian (added v5.2.1/5.2.2)

**Tafaseer (16+):**
- Text: Tafseer Taqi Usmani, Abul Ala Moudodi (Maududi), Ibne Kaseer (Ibn Kathir), Ahsan-ul-Biyan (newly added)
- Audio: Dr. Israr Ahmed, Mufti M. Sayeed
- Fahm-ul-Quran (Quranic comprehension through grammar)

**Audio Reciters (12+):**
- Sheikh As-Sudais, Sheikh Al-Shuraim, Qari Abdul Basit, Saad El Ghamidi, Sheikh Hani Ar-Rifai, Sheikh Ahmed Al Ajmi, Qari Ubaid-ur-Rehman (newly added)

**Search:**
- By Arabic word (searches original text)
- By English, Urdu, Roman Urdu, Hindi keyword (searches translations)
- By Arabic root word (alphabetical list)
- By Subject/Topic (alphabetical index in English and Urdu)
- Voice search

**Personal Tools:**
- Bookmarks — save position or mark important Ayaat
- Notes — add personal notes alongside any Ayah
- Sharing — share Ayah with custom backgrounds ("Flashes/Revelations")
- Night Mode
- Verse of the Moment / Verse of the Day

### HADITH

**Primary Books (7 — Kutub al-Sittah + Musnad Ahmed):**
1. Sahih Al-Bukhari
2. Sahih Muslim
3. Jame Tirmazi (Jami at-Tirmidhi)
4. Sunnan Abu Dawood
5. Sunnan Nisai (Sunan an-Nasa'i)
6. Sunnan Ibne Maja (Sunan Ibn Majah)
7. Musnad Ahmed

**Secondary / Expanded Books:**
- Al-Silsila-tus-Sahiha
- Mishkat-ul-Masabeeh
- Sahih Ibn Hibban
- Musannif Ibn Shayba
- Sahih Ibn Khuzaymah
- Sunan al-Darimi
- Muwatta Imam Malik
- Majma Al-Zawa'id
- Mustadrik Al-Hakim
- Sunnan-e-Beyhaqi (recently added)

**Total:** 78,000+ Ahadith

**Per-Hadith Content:**
- Original Arabic text
- Urdu translation
- English translation
- International reference number (Darus Salaam numbering)
- Baab (chapter title)
- Authenticity grade: Sahih (authentic) / Zaeef (weak)
- Source reference for the grade
- Takhreej (chain reference)
- Wazahat (contextual clarification)

**Navigation:**
- Browse chapter by chapter (as in original books)
- Filter by Sahih / Zaeef
- Keyword search across all books
- Auto Language Detection (Arabic, Urdu, Roman Urdu, English)
- Roman Urdu Hadith Search
- Bookmarks
- Audio recitation for select collections

## Known User-Reported Issues

1. **Navigation complexity** — app has deep nested menus; developers recommend YouTube tutorials
2. **Urdu-first default** — defaults to Urdu Tafseer; non-Urdu users must manually change language settings
3. **15-line Quran lag** — app can lag and jump back pages while scrolling the 15-line Quran on some devices
4. **Audio not working** — occasional recitation/Tafseer audio failures; fix is to check audio settings and restart
5. **iPad Tafseer glitch** — glitches when switching between Tafaseer on iPad
6. **Hadith bookmark navigation** — after bookmarking a Hadith, returning to the book to continue reading requires extra steps
7. **Ibadat content Urdu-only** — the Ibadat tab (Namaz, Roza, etc.) is Urdu-only; English version requested
8. **Recurring donation confusion** — some users accidentally set up monthly recurring donations; support can cancel
